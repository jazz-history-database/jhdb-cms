Featured People

Jack McCarthy

I am a longtime Philadelphia-area archivist and historian. I have held leadership positions at several area historical organizations and directed a number of major archives and public history projects, including projects for the Historical Society of Pennsylvania, Philadelphia Orchestra, Mann Music Center, Jazz Bridge, and other cultural organizations. My experience ranges from direct hands-on work with historical collections to broader based institutional strategic planning and project management. I am particularly interested in Philadelphia music history, Philadelphia business & industry history, and Northeast Philadelphia history.

Suzanne Cloud

Suzanne Cloud is an American jazz singer and the founding executive director of Jazz Bridge, an award-winning organization incorporated in 2005 with friend and fellow jazz singer Wendy Simon to help local jazz and blues musicians in crisis.[1] Cloud was named a Creative Connector[2] by the Philadelphia Leadership Council in 2012 and was featured by WHYY for her work for the organization.[3] Cloud is the editor of The Real Philadelphia Book, 2nd edition(being published by Temple University Press, Fall 2018), featuring over 300 original jazz and blues compositions by Philadelphia-area musicians, which was the dream of musician David Dzubinski of creating a compendium of music by regional artists.[4]
